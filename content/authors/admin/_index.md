Hello! Welcome to my website.

I am a fifth-year PhD candidate in Economics, a [2026-2027 Rackham Predoctoral Fellow](https://rackham.umich.edu/funding/rackham-predoctoral-fellowship-program/) and previously a [Lawrence Klein Fellow in Econometrics](https://lsa.umich.edu/econ/doctoral-program/awards---fellowships/lawrence-klein-fellowship-in-econometrics.html) in the Department of Economics at the University of Michigan-Ann Arbor. My research interests are in causal inference, treatment choice, and nonparametric welfare analysis. I use tools from statistical optimal transport, high dimensional statistics, and nonparametric and semiparametric statistics to address empirically relevant limitations of methods used across these domains.
